About Me - William Palmer

I have been making yardage books for myself for about a year, and a common problem I found at my local courses were the lack of yardage books.

Some of the cheap, 3D graphical books found in some pro shops had too much fluff for my liking. So, my goal was to go out and create a book that looks similar to the yardage books used on Tour, but also make it usable for the everyday amateur.

Keeping it simple, but also keeping a level of Tour professionalism is my goal and I hope to achieve that with these yardage books. I have certainly enjoyed using them and I hope you, too, enjoy Palmer Yardage Books.
